How to use these prompts

New to Claude? Start here 👋

Claude is a free AI assistant made by Anthropic. You don't need to install anything — it runs entirely in your browser. Here's how to use any prompt from this page in 4 steps.

1

Go to claude.ai — it's completely free to sign up with your email or Google account. No credit card needed.

2

Click "New conversation" to open a fresh chat with Claude.

3

Open any prompt pack below, copy the prompt you want, and paste it into the Claude chat box. Then fill in the brackets — replace things like [your course name] or [exam date] with your actual information.

4

Hit enter and Claude will respond with a structured, personalised output. You can follow up with questions or ask it to adjust anything — Claude remembers the full conversation.

🌿

Tip: The more detail you give Claude, the better the output. Don't just write "plan my week" — tell it your courses, your hours, your deadlines, and your goals. Specific input = specific, useful plan.
